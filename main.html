Ojai

From SB: 1.5 hrs Feb highs: 64¬∞F Sunset: 5:38 PM Vibe: Cozy romantic

The Draw

Small valley tucked between the Topatopa Mountains, 1.5h south of SB. Built around wellness, art, and slowing down. Orange groves, mountain views, boutique shops, galleries, year-round spa culture. Famous for the "Pink Moment" ‚Äî a sunset phenomenon where the mountains glow pink and lavender. Cool enough to be cozy, warm enough to be outside all day.

  • Wine tasting at Ojai Vineyard (since 1983) and olive oil at Ojai Olive Oil Co.
  • Bart's Books ‚Äî famous outdoor bookstore since 1964
  • Ojai Valley Trail ‚Äî paved path through orchards and mountains

  • Nocciola ‚Äî excellent Italian, indoor and patio, one block off main street

  • Rory's Place ‚Äî cozy fine dining, seafood, crafted cocktails
