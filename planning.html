Flights add too much stress for this trip ‚Äî driving to LAX, security, timing ‚Äî all of that kills the "taken care of" vibe I'm going for. We have a car and we're in one of the best road trip corridors in California, so it makes way more sense to drive.

Why I'm skipping the other options: SLC is cold and skiing before her midterm is risky energy-wise.
